Georgetown University Oncology Patient Support

Georgetown University Oncology Patient Support (GU OPS) is an organization founded on the principle of Cura Personalis, or care for the whole person, and its role in medicine. We are a group that volunteers in the Lombardi Cancer Center through a partnership with the Arts and Humanities Program. We are focused on supporting patients in all ways possible, particularly by helping them use art as a therapeutic and creative outlet.
