Our Mission

We are focused on improving the patient experience through the use of art as a therapeutic and creative outlet.

Programs

Our team of student volunteers works to provide daily crafting activities, including card-making and origmai, for visitors of the Lombardi Comprehensive Cancer Center. We also hand-make seasonal decorations for the clinic throughout the year. 

Click here to see our work!

History

We are founded on the principle of Cura Personalis, or care for the whole person, and its role in medicine. Our program began through the generous donations of patient Marge Allen and her husband, Bill Allen.

Our Partners

We volunteer in the Georgetown Lombardi Comprehensive Cancer Center through a partnership with the Arts and Humanities Program. We are sponsored by the Center for Social Justice at Georgetown University.
