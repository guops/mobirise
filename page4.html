Interested in volunteering? Join our team!

1

Reach out

Any student affiliated with Georgetown University is eligible to volunteer. Get in touch with one of our board members who will help you with next steps.

2

On-boarding

Review our "Code of Conduct & Expectations." Complete neccessary forms and meet with our incredible team for an orientation of the space and organization.

3

Volunteer

Sign up for a 2-hour shift each week and attend seasonal decorating events throughout the year. Promote health & wellness, meet other volunteers, and share your artwork!
